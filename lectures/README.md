## ‚ö†Ô∏è Safety Reminders

**Always prioritize safety when working with electronics:**

- Never work with AC mains voltage unless properly trained
- Use appropriate fuses and circuit breakers
- Discharge capacitors before handling
- Work in a well-ventilated area (especially when soldering)
- Wear safety glasses when soldering or cutting leads
- Keep fire extinguisher nearby
- Understand current ratings and power dissipation
- Use proper insulation and avoid short circuits
- Start with low voltages (5V, 9V, 12V DC)
- Get professional help for high-voltage or high-current projects
